PDFnotes values your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information Collection and Use

  • PDFnotes runs entirely in your browser.
  • All collected text snippets remain stored locally on your device until you export them as a PDF.
  • We do not transmit your data to any server or third party.

Permissions

  • activeTab, host permissions, notifications and scripting are used solely to read and capture the text you select on webpages you visit.
  • storage is used only to save your selected text locally until you choose to export it.

User Responsibility

  • All content collected with PDFnotes should be referenced to the original source.
  • Do not present collected content as your own without proper attribution.
